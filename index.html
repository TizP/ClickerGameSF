Tutorial: How to Play

Welcome to the Salesforce Pipeline Simulator! Your goal is to build a massive sales pipeline and reach $1 Billion in revenue.

Core Resources:

  • Leads: The raw potential. Generated by clicking the left button or buying Lead Generation Teams. Spent on Lead Teams, some upgrades, and customer acquisition.
  • Opportunities (Opps): Qualified potential. Generated by clicking the right button or buying Opportunity Generation Teams. Spent on Opp Teams, some upgrades, and customer acquisition.
  • Customers: The source of revenue! Acquired by spending Leads & Opportunities.
  • Money ($): Your score! Generated automatically by your Customers. Spent on powerful efficiency upgrades.

Generating Resources:

  • Manual Clicks: Click the Lead and Opportunity buttons in the center to get started. Upgrades can increase the amount you get per click.
  • Teams & Features (Right Panel): Buy Teams (like SDRs, BDRs) and Features (like Pardot Campaigns, Integration Hubs) to automatically generate Leads and/or Opportunities per second (L/S, O/S). Their costs increase with each purchase.

Getting Customers (Acquisition):

  • The game automatically tries to convert Leads and Opportunities into Customers.
  • Acquisition Cost: Each attempt costs a specific amount of Leads AND Opportunities (shown in the center stats). This cost increases as you get more customers, but can be reduced with upgrades.
  • Acquisition Rate: How many attempts are made per second. Increase this with upgrades to convert faster.
  • Acquisition Success: The percentage chance that an attempt actually results in a new Customer. Increase this with upgrades. Failure still costs resources!
  • Pause Acquisition: Use the button in the top bar if you need to save up Leads/Opps without spending them on attempts.

Generating Money:

  • Each Customer generates Money passively based on the Customer Value Rate (CVR).
  • Increase your CVR with specific upgrades to earn Money faster.

Upgrades & Training (Left Panel):

  • Spend your resources here on various upgrades to:
    • Increase manual click power.
    • Boost the output of specific Teams/Features (flat amount, percentage, or multiplier).
    • Improve overall Team/Feature efficiency.
    • Increase Acquisition Rate or Success Chance.
    • Decrease Acquisition Cost.
    • Increase Customer Value Rate (CVR).
    • Gain powerful bonuses by spending Customers themselves!

Goal & Saving:

  • Keep building your pipeline until you reach $1,000,000,000 (1 Billion)!
  • The game auto-saves every 30 seconds. You can also save manually or delete your save using the top bar buttons.

Good luck building your pipeline!
